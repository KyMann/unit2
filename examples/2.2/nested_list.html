Nested List Example

This is an example of a nested list. We will explore the hobbies of a 4 person family.

  1. Mom's Hobbies
    • Jogging
    • Fishing
    • Reading
  2. Dad's Hobbies
    • Hunting
    • Football
    • Baking
  3. Daughter's Hobbies
    • Skiing
    • Driving
    • Shoping
  4. Son's Hobbies
    • Soccer
    • Video Games
    • Piano
